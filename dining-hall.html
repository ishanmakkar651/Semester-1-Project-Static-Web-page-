Dining Hall

The Dining Hall at Chitkara is more than just a place to eat; it's a community. Gather with friends over a meal and enjoy a diverse menu crafted by our talented chefs who bring flavors from all over the world.

Meet Our Chefs

Our chefs are the heart of the dining hall. Their dedication to quality and taste make every meal special. Here are a few of the people who make it all happen:

  • Chef Raj - Head Chef, specializing in North Indian cuisine
  • Chef Priya - Sous Chef, Italian and Continental dishes
  • Chef Anil - Pastry Chef, desserts and baked goods
